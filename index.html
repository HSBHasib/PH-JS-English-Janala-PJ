Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner and follow the registration process.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, our website is completely free for all learners. You can access all basic lessons, vocabulary lists, and practice tools without any subscription fees.
3. Do I need to create an account?
While you can browse some lessons as a guest, we recommend creating a free account to track your progress, save favorite words, and participate in quizzes.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
The best way is to use our 'Vocabulary' section daily. Listen to the pronunciations, learn the meanings, and try to use those words in your own sentences.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes! Once you successfully complete all the lessons and pass the final assessment of a specific level, you will be eligible for a digital certificate.
